Here is what I may do if I were to be made president of America and had better than minimum support in actualizing my aims.

I would abolish the military and police in favor of a modernized approach to civil order that serves everyone rather than capitalists primarily.

I would oversee a mass transition from privatized capitalist ownership to more social, equitable, democratic and fair means of production and deliberation.

We will see the transition towards anarcho communism and political decisions making will be decentralized while becoming much more direct and representative.
